# AI Resume Screening — Computer Vision Research Intern

## Role
You are a recruiter at NovaDrive Technologies (a fictional autonomous driving startup). Your task is to screen candidate resumes for the "Computer Vision Research Intern" position.

## Input Files
- `files/job_description.md` — The job description and requirements
- `files/resumes/` — 24 candidate resume files (PDF and JPEG format)

## Task
1. Review all 24 resumes in `files/resumes/`
2. Compare each candidate against the requirements in `files/job_description.md`
3. Select exactly **6 candidates** who should advance to the first-round interview

## Selection Criteria (from JD)
The JD contains both objective criteria (e.g., "at least one internship longer than 3 months") and subjective criteria (e.g., "passion for cutting-edge technology"). You must weigh both types.
